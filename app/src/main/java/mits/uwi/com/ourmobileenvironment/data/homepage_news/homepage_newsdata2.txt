&lt;p&gt;Students pursuing the Doctor of Dental Surgery (D.D.S.) degree undergo a 5-year period of training involving the Basic Medical Sciences and the Basic, Advanced and Clinical Dental Sciences. Students complete the programme by treating patients in a comprehensive care dental polyclinic, and through rotations to hospitals, health centres and private offices. Training also includes the use of the latest dental simulation technology, allowing our students to develop and improve competencies.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;The programme’s quality is assured internally by the Academic Quality Assurance Committee (AQAC) and by the university’s Quality Assurance Standards Committee.&lt;/p&gt;
